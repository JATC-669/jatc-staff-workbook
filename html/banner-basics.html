  • Student Form - basic viewing of one student's infomration
    • ID number must be entered to bring up record
      • Enter directly: @00------
      • Search: Have field selected, press F9
        • Enter data in chosen field(s)
        • % acts as a wildcard. It can be used in front of, or at the end of a string, or both
        • Press F8 to execute search
        • Double click on result to bring up record
      • After bringing up record, click in Address field to populate fields and allow selection of tabs
  • Tabs:
    • Info: Basic information about the student and their program
    • Program: ONly used to add or remove courses from their required program. DO NOT alter without Josh or JATC direction.
    • Enrollment: Basic infomration about the student's progress in their courses.
        On the enrollment tab you can:
      • Enroll in a course
      • create a final exam
      • complete a course/unenroll form course
      • View current, past, and future courses
      • View grades
      • View enrollment dates
      • If/when a final exam was requested or mailed
      • View individual lesson grades by clicking on the details button for a specific course
      • Many of these fields can be manually overwritten
    • Adjustments: We typically don't use this
    • Comments: ALWAYS check the comments if interaction directly with an apprentice or dealing with some sort of anomaly. Always leave a comment if making any kind of change that we might need to know when or why it occurec (you'll learn what constitutes that type of change as you go.)
    • Reports: Most student-specific, automatically-generated printed matter can be created in ths tab, including scantrons and welcome letters
    • Orders: Most orders are placed when enrolling students. But if they lose their materials or we have some other reason to order a book, it can be done here.
    • Enrollment History: Good to see an apprentice's oldest course if the Enrollment tab is a bit confusing. This lists the courses in order of when they were enrolled by date
